Commercial Law

Commercial lawyers for business in Victoria.

Good legal foundations let a business grow with confidence. We advise founders, family businesses and established companies on structuring, shareholder and partnership agreements, commercial contracts, leasing, employment and the sale or purchase of a business, getting the detail right so the deal holds up long after it is signed.

When a commercial relationship breaks down, we protect your position. We act in contract and shareholder disputes, debt recovery, leasing and construction disputes and restraint of trade claims, and because we handle both the deal and the dispute, our advice always keeps one eye on how an agreement performs if it is ever tested.

Family Law

Family lawyers for separation, parenting and property.

Separation affects your children, your home and your financial future all at once. Our Melbourne family lawyers help you reach practical parenting and property arrangements, formalise them as consent orders or binding financial agreements, and move forward with certainty. Where matters can be settled sensibly, we settle them, and spare you the cost and strain of court.

When agreement is not possible, you want someone steady and firm in your corner. We represent you in parenting and property disputes, urgent applications, intervention orders and spousal maintenance, always with discretion and a clear sense of what matters most to you and your family.

Wills & Estates

Wills and estates lawyers in Melbourne.

A clear, valid will is the simplest way to protect the people you love. We prepare wills, testamentary trusts, powers of attorney and advance care directives for individuals and families across Victoria, then guide your executor through probate and estate administration when the time comes. We make estate planning straightforward, and keep your documents current as your life changes.

When an estate is contested, we act with the same care and resolve. We advise on family provision claims under Part IV, challenges to a will's validity, and disputes between executors and beneficiaries. Strict time limits apply to many estate claims, so the sooner you speak with us, the more we can do.
