The principal

Meet Spencer Alexander.

Spencer Alexander
Principal

Spencer Alexander is a Melbourne lawyer who believes good advice begins with listening. For more than a decade he has sat across the table from people on some of the hardest days of their lives, and what they tend to remember afterwards is not just the law, but the feeling of being genuinely heard and looked after — and of knowing that someone fought as hard as their case could be fought, gave them every chance of success, and was, from first call to last, completely on their side.

He was drawn to the law for a simple reason: he wanted to help people through the moments that feel overwhelming. A loss in the family. The end of a relationship. A business under real pressure. Lifting some of that weight off a person's shoulders, and fighting for the best possible outcome for every client, is his core motivation.

His experience runs deep. Over more than ten years he has practised across Commercial Law, Family Law and Wills and Estates, both internationally and throughout the private sector here at home. It is a breadth of perspective and experience that is rare in a boutique firm, and it shows in advice that is commercially grounded, legally rigorous and delivered in plain language.

He started Spencer Alexander Lawyers because he had seen too many people passed between junior staff and left in the dark about their own matter. Here it is different. You deal with him directly, from the first phone call through to the last, and your matter is given the seniority, discretion and genuine care it deserves.

Away from the desk he is a Melburnian through and through. The values he was raised on, honesty, loyalty and looking after the people who rely on you, are the same ones he brings to every client he acts for.

Legal excellence is the core value of his practice.

General Manager & Compliance

Meet Jordyn Kincaid.

Jordyn Kincaid
General Manager & Compliance Specialist

Jordyn Kincaid is the engine room of Spencer Alexander Lawyers. As General Manager and Compliance Specialist he runs the business of the firm — its systems, its scheduling, its standards and its compliance obligations — so that every matter moves on time and every lawyer's attention stays exactly where it belongs: on the client.

He brings extensive experience managing companies of every scale — from small businesses to multi-million-dollar operations — and it shows in how the firm runs day to day. He is the reason calls are returned promptly, deadlines never drift, and every fee estimate is confirmed in writing before work begins. Organised, personable and quietly exacting, Jordyn holds the firm to the same test our lawyers hold themselves to — clear answers, kept promises, no surprises.

Clients feel his work most in the things that never happen: the appointment that never clashes, the document that never goes missing, the invoice that never arrives unexplained. Behind the scenes he builds and maintains the systems that let a boutique firm deliver big-firm reliability — and the follow-through that keeps every promise made in a meeting.

For Jordyn, good management is a form of client care. When the machinery of a firm runs quietly, clients get what they actually came for: their lawyer's full attention.

Client relations & accounts

Meet Katalin Csapo.

Katalin Csapo
Client Relations & Accounts Manager

Katalin Csapo is the first voice many clients hear, and the reason working with the firm feels effortless from that moment on. As Client Relations & Accounts Manager she looks after the people side of the practice — welcoming new clients, keeping you informed at every step, and making sure the accounts side of your matter is always clear.

She brings extensive experience managing accounts for companies large and small, together with the kind of interpersonal skill that cannot be taught — warm, unflappable and precise. Whether your question is about your matter, your invoice or simply what happens next, Katalin will have the answer, or find it fast.

Legal matters move through unfamiliar territory, and Katalin makes sure no client walks it alone. She checks in before you need to ask, translates process into plain language, and treats every file as a relationship rather than a transaction — which is why so many clients mention her by name.

On the accounts side she is meticulous: estimates confirmed, invoices itemised and explained, questions answered the same day. It is the quiet discipline that makes the firm's promise of no surprises real.
